Garudhiya – Traditional Maldivian Fish Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 kg fresh tuna or reef fish (cut into chunks)
  • 2 L cold water
  • ½ tsp sea salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 2–3 green chilies, slit
  • Fresh curry leaves & a few sprigs of cilantro

Directions

  1. Bring water to a gentle boil in a large pot.
  2. Add the fish pieces, salt, onion, tomatoes, and chilies.
  3. Simmer 12‑15 minutes until the fish is cooked through.
  4. Stir in curry leaves and cilantro just before turning off the heat.
  5. Serve hot with steamed rice or flatbread.
