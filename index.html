Garudhiya – Maldivian Clear Fish Broth

Ingredients (4 servings)

Directions

  1. Show/Hide Prep the fish

    Rinse the fish pieces under cold water and pat dry. Set aside.

  2. Show/Hide Boil the base

    In a large pot, bring 2 L water to a gentle boil. Add sliced onion, garlic, ginger, green chilies, peppercorns, and cardamom (if using). Simmer 5 min.

  3. Show/Hide Add the fish

    Gently lower the fish chunks into the simmering broth. Reduce heat to low; keep a gentle simmer.

  4. Show/Hide Season

    Sprinkle salt to taste. Optional: a splash of lime juice for brightness.

  5. Show/Hide Cook briefly

    Cook 8‑10 min until fish turns opaque and flakes easily. Do not over‑boil.

  6. Show/Hide Finish & Serve

    Turn off heat. Ladle broth into bowls with a generous piece of fish. Garnish with fresh curry leaves or cilantro if desired. Serve with steamed rice, lime wedges, and optional fried onions.
